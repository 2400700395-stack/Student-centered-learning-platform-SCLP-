STUDENT-CENTERED LEARNING PLATFORM

Description

The Student-Centered Learning platform(SCLP) is a digital initiative designed to transform education in Uganda by empowering the Learners and educators through personalised, interactive and collaborative learning experiences. SCLP leverages Technology to put students at the heart of the learning process, fostering creativity and critical thinking relevant to Uganda's education and aspirations.

Vision

To be the best educative platform in Uganda and in the world where every learner can thrive through tailored, engaging and student-driven education pathways,contributing to individual and national development.

Mission

To empower educators and learners in Uganda with innovative, Student-Centered tools and approaches that promote personalised learning, enhance academic outcomes, and nurture skills for lifelong success in global context.
